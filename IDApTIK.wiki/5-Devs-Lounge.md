## 🏗 Development Approach  

**Hybrid-Prototyping-First with Evolutionary Scaling**:  
🔥 **Core mechanics are prototyped early**, ensuring rapid validation before deeper optimization.  
🔥 **Iterative refinement follows structured phases**, scaling asymmetric multiplayer organically.
