# Development Roadmap  

IDApTIK evolves in structured phases, ensuring mechanics are tested, refined, and stress-tested before moving forward.  

## **Envelope (v0.1) – Minimum Viable Game**  
This revision aims to produce the first playable version of Gitpoint (potentially with a new name) as a TypeScript platformer, establishing the foundation of its **gameplay loop and asymmetric mechanics**.  

### **Goals for Envelope (v0.1)**  
✅ **Core platforming mechanics** – Movement, jumping, crawling, object interaction.  
✅ **Basic hacker-player interactions** – Opening doors, timing elevators, activating security overrides.  
✅ **Game documentation in wiki pages** – Clarifying mechanics, controls, and gameplay flow.  
✅ **First set of sprite artwork** – Enough to convey essential mechanics visually.  
✅ **Devlog for Envelope (v0.1)** – Tracking progress and setting up **next steps** for Wheel.  

## **Upcoming Development Phases**  
### **Wheel – Refining Core Movement & Interaction**  
- Improving stealth responsiveness, collision handling, and precision controls.  
- Expanding hacker capabilities: **security loop manipulation, timed overrides, sabotage elements.**  
- Early AI behaviors for environmental hazards and dynamic detection.  

### **Skateboard – Deepening Asymmetric Multiplayer**  
- Larger level structures with multi-phase infiltration objectives.  
- Developing more **hacker-based control systems**, moving beyond static activation toggles.  
- Refining communication elements between infiltrator and hacker.  

### **Bicycle – Expanding Strategic Depth**  
- Adding **AI-led countermeasures** that react dynamically to player interference.  
- Implementing level designs that encourage **problem-solving through cooperation.**  

### **Chassis – Strengthening Interactive Complexity**  
- Improved environmental mechanics: **terrain effects, sound sensitivity, player misdirection tools.**  
- Testing first **non-linear infiltration missions**, encouraging multiple paths of entry.  

### **Motorcycle – The Full Experience**  
- Expanding sabotage mechanics—rogue hacker interference and **adaptable security AI.**  
- Perfecting emergent gameplay interactions that **create tension dynamically.**  
- Final multiplayer framework for **high-stakes asymmetric infiltration**.  

### **Computer Game – Beyond Proof of Concept**  
- Once the core experience is complete, transitioning into **polished, expanded iterations**.  
- Strengthening gameplay adaptability based on **contributor feedback and structured testing.**
